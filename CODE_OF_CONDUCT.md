# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Treat other participants with respect and keep technical disagreement focused on the work.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, intimidation, deliberate disruption, disclosure of another person's private information, and retaliation are not accepted.

## Scope

This standard applies in project repositories, issue trackers, reviews, discussions, and other spaces where someone represents the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

For ordinary disagreements, first ask clearly for the behavior to stop or step away and seek maintainer help. For sensitive conduct concerns, contact a maintainer privately using the contact information on their GitHub profile. Do not put private details in a public issue.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently exclude participants whose behavior is harmful. Reports will be handled as privately as practical, with information shared only as needed to understand and address the concern. Good-faith reports and participation in an investigation must not result in retaliation.

Project maintainers are responsible for applying this policy fairly. A maintainer with a conflict of interest should recuse themselves from the report.
